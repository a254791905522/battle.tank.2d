Battle Tank Arena 2D

Fast top-down tank action

Free iOS 15+ Simulation Ages 9+

Drive into fast 2D arena battles where precision, timing, and strategy decide every fight. Battle Tank Arena 2D is a tactical arcade tank shooter: dodge enemy fire, collect upgrades, and survive dynamic arenas filled with obstacles, power-ups, and intense projectile combat across grassland, desert, snow, and volcanic battlefields.

Combat basics

  1. Move & grapple — Use your grappling hook to zip across the arena, dodge shots, and pull enemies into danger.
  2. Deflect with timing — Activate your shield at the right moment to bounce projectiles back at attackers.
  3. Power up & ram — Collect power orbs for a red strength shield, then slam enemy tanks with physics-based knockback.
  4. Adapt your loadout — Switch between fast movement mode and heavy tank mode to match each arena challenge.

Grappling hook combat
