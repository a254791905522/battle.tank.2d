Battle Tank Arena 2D — Privacy

How this tank arena game handles information on your device and through the App Store.

No data collected No account required Offline-capable play

App Store summary: The listing for Battle Tank Arena 2D states that the developer does not collect data from you through the app, as Apple defines “collection.” If that label changes after an update, the App Store privacy section is the authoritative source.

▣

Section A

Local gameplay storage

Arena progress, mode preferences, and other session data needed to run the game are stored on your iPhone or iPad inside the app’s sandbox. This keeps your battles available when you return without requiring a separate login.

Deleting the app or clearing its data in iOS Settings removes that local information from your device.

◎

Section B

No developer data collection

Per the App Store “App Privacy” disclosure, Battle Tank Arena 2D is labeled as not collecting user data through the app. The game is designed as a self-contained arcade experience without asking you to register an in-game account.

◈

Section C

Apple’s platform services

When you download, update, or purchase through the App Store, Apple processes those transactions and related device information under Apple’s own privacy policy. That processing is outside this game’s direct control.

Device diagnostics, iCloud backups (if enabled), and system-level settings follow Apple’s policies and your iOS privacy choices.

⚑

Section D

Players aged 9 and up

The game is rated 9+ for infrequent cartoon or fantasy violence. Parents and guardians can use Apple’s Screen Time and parental controls to manage installs, purchases, and device sharing for younger players.

↻

Section E

Future updates

If a later version adds online leaderboards, analytics, advertising, or account features that change how data is handled, this page will be revised to describe those practices. Always check the installed build and the current App Store privacy label first.

Your controls

Uninstall Battle Tank Arena 2D to remove its local app data. For Apple ID and system privacy, open Settings → Privacy & Security on your device.

Legal scope

This page is a plain-language overview for players, not legal advice. It does not replace Apple’s App Store terms or mandatory disclosures in your jurisdiction.

If anything here conflicts with the installed app or the App Store “App Privacy” section, rely on the store listing and on-device behavior first.
